 ThreadSafeQueue (ThreadSafeQueue &&)=delete
 Disable move constructor. Although std::mutex and std::condition_variable technically have move operations deleted, even if they were movable, transferring them between ThreadSafeQueue instances would break synchronization guarantees. To enforce strict ownership, movement is disabled.
